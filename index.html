What is this bootcamp about?

Reinforcement learning (RL) is a powerful learning paradigm of machine learning (ML). Reinforcement Learning (RL) is at the cutting edge of artificial intelligence, powering advancements in robotics, autonomous systems, game playing, and decision-making algorithms. Mastering RL opens up a world of opportunities in both research and industry, enabling you to contribute to groundbreaking innovations.

Capturing your control problem as a meaningful Markov Decision Process (MDP) is not trivial. Additional challenges arise in the training in terms of stability and evaluation. Other practical aspects include reproducibility, efficiency, implementation, deployment in hardware, or choosing the most suitable algorithm for your problem.

RL applications in particle accelerators are very promising, but have been deployed in real machines only a handful of times. Who should attend?

This bootcamp is ideal for students, professionals, and enthusiasts with a basic understanding of machine learning who are looking to deepen their knowledge in reinforcement learning.

Expert-led sessions: Learn from leading experts in the field with real-world experience in applying RL to solve complex problems.

Hands-on projects: Dive deep into RL concepts with hands-on projects that simulate real-world challenges.

Collaborative learning environment: Engage with peers who share your passion for AI and RL.

State-of-the-art techniques: Stay ahead of the curve with lessons on the latest RL algorithms, tools, and best practices.

Stunning Salzburg setting: Allow the beauty of Salzburg to inspire your learning journey and fuel your creativity.

Participation in the workshop is free of charge
